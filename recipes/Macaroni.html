Kraft Mac N Cheese

Description

The best food in the world, Kraft Mac N' Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 box Kraft Mac N Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk (whole is best, but any will do!)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add noods once the water is boiling
  3. When noods are nice and soft, throw a noodle as hard as possible at your roommate, family member, or dog (If you dont have one of these, a neighbor will do)
  4. Add milk, butter, and cheese powder packet
  5. Enjoy the greatest meal ever straight out of heavens front door!
